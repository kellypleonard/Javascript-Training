Welcome to Safe Cracker! Discover the three-digit combination before running out of guesses. In the feedback list at the bottom, the value highlighted in green (e.g, 2) represents the number of digits in your guess that are in the combination and in the correct place. The value highlighted in yellow (e.g., 1) represents the number of digits in your guess that are in the combination but in the wrong place. So, your goal is to get to 3 0, which means your guess is identical to the combination.
